# Services

Nina builds bespoke digital infrastructure. Her services are tailored to businesses that require custom solutions rather than off-the-shelf products.

## Custom Web Applications
For problems that cannot be solved with a template. These are tailored platforms—booking systems, client portals, internal dashboards—built from the ground up to fit a specific workflow.
*   **Focus:** Speed, reliability, and exact fit.
*   **Result:** A tool that adapts to the business, not the other way around.

## AI Automation Workflows
Connecting disconnected tools to create seamless operations. This involves orchestrating data between emails, CRMs, project management tools, and AI models.
*   **Focus:** Eliminating manual data entry and repetitive admin tasks.
*   **Result:** Processes that run automatically, 24/7.

## Intelligent Assistants
Custom AI agents designed to handle specific domains of knowledge or tasks. Unlike generic chatbots, these are context-aware tools trained on specific business data to answer client queries, route information, or assist internal teams.
*   **Focus:** Accuracy, tone, and specific utility.
*   **Result:** Scalable communication that feels personal and helpful.

## System Architecture
Advisory and design for digital ecosystems. Evaluating existing tool stacks, identifying bottlenecks, and designing a roadmap for a more efficient, automated future.
*   **Focus:** Long-term stability and scalability.
*   **Result:** A clear plan for digital maturity.
